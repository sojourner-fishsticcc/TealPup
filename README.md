Porting Stock KSP Mods to RO/RP-1

I personally like the challenge of RO, but I find the lack of part models very difficult for someone coming from the stock game. This mod aims to port some mods from the stock game (mostly BluedogDesignBureau and Tantares,) to be compatible with RO's scaling and systems and RP-1's progression.

This is my first time creating a mod!! I have a long history of writing my own custom configs for personal use, but have never attempted to release anything publicly. Please be patient with my stupid ass <3
